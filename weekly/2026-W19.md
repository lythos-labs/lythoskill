# Week 19, 2026 (May 1 – May 7)

## TL;DR

The week's center of gravity was **cold-pool foundation extraction** — what started as an emergency-lane patch (`deck add` failing E2E quick-start on real-world skill repos) was diagnosed mid-week as an architectural defect: deck/curator/arena each ran raw `git clone`, no service layer between controllers and DAO. The fix was rescoped from "patch quick-start" to "extract `@lythos/cold-pool` as the dedicated resource-holder package, enforcing FQ-only locators and intent/plan/execute pattern uniformly". One session shipped T1-T13: new package, types, resolver, fetch primitives, deck migration, CI validation. Three follow-up policy ADRs sedimented (prune-as-heredoc, refresh-discover-then-apply, validate-companion pattern). 0.9.24→0.9.25→0.9.26 iterative npm publishes corrected two defects (workspace:* literal + deck-add-monorepo-exit-fail). The most-mattering single decision: **localhost is just another host segment** ("FQ locator is path; one level deeper = one segment of the locator"). It removed all special-case branching from the codebase and fixed the trigger of the morning's CPTSD-induced skill-deck.toml overwrite.

## Project Lesson Candidates (promote to wiki on second occurrence)

1. **Emergent E2E findings should be allowed to rescope mid-week.** The agent-spawn epic's `deck link` instability was the canary that motivated the cold-pool surgery. Rigid week plans would have suppressed the signal.
2. **Post-compaction agent drift is a first-class failure mode, not noise.** The morning's `skill-deck.toml` overwrite + the `~/.agents/skill-repos/{copy-polish,web-search}` legacy entries are both real artifacts of past CPTSD episodes. Heredoc-only policies + cleanup scripts surface drift without compounding it.
3. **Self-validating artifacts beat external assertions.** The stub repo's "congrats you found me right" pattern + `cortex probe`'s status-vs-Status-History check are instances of a general pattern: embed reality-check claims into the artifact itself. Generalized as ADR-20260507110332831 (validate-companion).
4. **Migration commits with ✅ migrated + ❌ preserved make boundaries auditable.** Confirmed 2026-05-07; saved as feedback memory. Applied across all T5–T11 commits this session.

## What I notice about my own thinking this week

The user (whose taste this codifies) trusts agents to act on smart-task-level confidence ≥9/10 if reversible + low-impact, but gates everything irreversible on explicit signal. Within that frame, the agent's job isn't to predict — it's to **calibrate evidence-grounded priors** (the decision-profile doc) and **make boundaries auditable** (commit messages, ADR rejected-alternatives, heredoc annotations). Direct emotional inference about user state is the failure mode (CPTSD agent pattern); evidence citation is the corrective.
